Dear Darlene

I created this because I want to apologize and send a message.

I've seen your story in Instagram, ginawa ko to dahil tama ka actions speak louder than words.

This is me taking action. Maybe this isn't enough, this bargain. Pero gusto ko lang ipahatid sayo na sorry sa katangahan ko.

Alam ko na paulit ulit nalang. Sa mag kakaibang kadahilanan.

At gayunpaman naiintindihan ko ang problem mo.

I'll stay. Kahit di mo man ako pansinin.

Dahil one thing is for sure. I really love you.

Sorry if I always bring out the pain. But I always stay. Im just waiting on you.

I do not know what you will feel in this letter.

Baka ma cringe ka dito or something. But it's you. When I imagine your face I smile

Dahil alam ko na may babaing akong napapagsabihan ng feelings ko.For that I fell safe and Loved.

Feel free to do whatever you want with this letter. I'll just wait for you.

My dearest apologies
Louise
